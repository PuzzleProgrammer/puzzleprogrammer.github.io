What is purposesocial?

We are a web development social enterprise.

    Website Development..

  • PurposeSocial employs the best practices in the industry to give you a superior performing website.

    • Our team makes sure your website attracts real customers.

  • Our team makes sure that your website has analytic features built-in (that you don’t have to worry about), so that your website is optimized to attract more traffic - and real customers!

    Junior Dev Accelerator..

  • We have a team that also works on IT projects that contribute to important social and academic research projects.
  • Our Junior Developers are mentored by a team of Senior Mentors who have extensive experience in the tech industry.
  • The Junior Dev team learns how to collaborate effectively on a technical team, while simultaneously building their portfolios so that they have real-world examples to show future employers.

    Social Enterprise..

  • PurposeSocial is committed to diversity in tech, and has a technical team comprised of at least 50% women and minorities at all times.
  • We give back to the community by providing mentorship and training via our Junior Dev Accelerator with a purpose.

    • PurposeSocial is committed to diversity and giving back to the community.

Erin Athene

Position:

Managing Partner

Unique Superpower:

Creating Opportunities and Market Demand

Experience:

Victoria Director for Lighthouse Labs; Partner in Hot Mama Health & Fitness Franchising Ltd., owner of 18 franchises empowering women across Canada. Erin provides training to entrepreneurs on Lean Startup methodology, Customer Discovery and Market Validation as an Advisor and Management Consultant to early stage companies.

Erin has reviewed over 500 business plans and CEO pitches for deal screening in angel investment groups, and been a panel judge for numerous pitching events, such as selecting companies for the Venture Acceleration Program while she was an advisor and Executive-in-Residence to early stage companies at VIATEC (Victoria Innovation, Advanced Technology & Entrepreneurship Council).
